About us

  • Ryan Harris

    Ryan Harris is an award-winning Pastry Chef & Consultant that has worked under world renown chefs with Michelin starred restaurants. He loves the alchemy behind creating food and pastry and is always exploring raw ingredients, materials, techniques and tools to create an experience that engages all the senses.

  • Shawn Aoki

    Shawn Aoki is a internationally accomplished Chef and Consultant that brings years of experience developing culinary concepts for global luxury hotel brands. After growing up within a family where food was a way of life; till to today, he chases a passion for learning, creating and sharing those encounters enveloped in the word of food.
